Frequently asked questions

How much does a stamped concrete patio cost in Dallas?

Most of our stamped patios run $14–$22 per square foot installed, depending on pattern, coloring method, and site prep. A typical 400 sq ft patio lands between $6,000 and $9,000 — we give exact fixed-price quotes after a free site visit.

How long does installation take?

Most residential patios are formed, poured, and stamped in 2–3 days, then sealed after a 7-day cure. Garage epoxy is typically a 2-day install with 24-hour walk-on time.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes — every installation includes a written 5-year workmanship warranty covering cracking beyond hairline, delamination, and sealer failure.
